Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

No data collection

Kaja is a local-first desktop application. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to Kaja or any third party. All data stays on your machine.

Service connections

When you configure connections to your own gRPC or Twirp services, the request data you enter is sent directly to those user-specified servers. Kaja does not intermediate, log, or store this traffic.

Analytics and tracking

Kaja does not include any analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can open an issue on GitHub.
